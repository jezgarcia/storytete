Don't Mess With the Head

The story starts in a very simple format.
A man requires a transplant. The difference comes from the part of the body that is required in this operation.
Normally you would assume that a kidney transplant was needed. Or maybe a heart transplant. But this was different.
The type of transplant needed was a head.
From all over the world, scientists and medical specialists were called over to embark in this endeavor.
Evenetually, they came up with a working plan.
But, it was flawed.
Their biggest problem was connecting the spinal cord.
They would only be able to connect it to the point that only 20% of it would be functional.
This gave enough for simple movement but not enough for intellectual thought.
It would leave the subject just like any other animal.
It would survive on instinct alone.
